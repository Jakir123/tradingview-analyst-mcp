# Stan Weinstein — Stage Analysis
**Best for:** Overall market trend identification, long-term investment, swing trade direction filter

## The Core Idea
Every stock goes through 4 stages in a cycle. The only time to BUY is Stage 2. The only time to SELL is when Stage 2 ends. Everything else is waiting.

---

## The 4 Stages

### Stage 1 — Basing / Accumulation
- Price is flat and moving sideways
- 30-week MA (150-day MA) is flat or just beginning to turn up
- Volume is low and irregular
- **Action: WAIT. Do not buy yet. Start watching.**
- **DSE signal:** Price oscillates in a 10-15% range for several weeks/months

### Stage 2 — Uptrend (THE ONLY BUY ZONE)
- Price breaks above Stage 1 resistance with increased volume
- Price is above the 30-week MA
- 30-week MA is rising
- Higher highs and higher lows on the chart
- **Action: BUY on the breakout or on pullbacks to the 30-week MA**
- **Entry trigger:** Close above Stage 1 resistance with volume at least 1.5x average
- **DSE signal:** Volume spike 1.5-2x on breakout day

### Stage 3 — Topping / Distribution
- Price becomes choppy and volatile near the highs
- 30-week MA starts to flatten
- Price crosses the MA multiple times in both directions
- Volume on down days starts to exceed volume on up days
- **Action: SELL or take profits. Do NOT buy.**
- **DSE signal:** Big volume on down bars, weak volume on up bars

### Stage 4 — Downtrend (NEVER BUY)
- Price breaks below Stage 3 support
- Price is below the 30-week MA
- 30-week MA is declining
- Lower lows and lower highs
- **Action: AVOID completely. If holding, SELL immediately.**
- **DSE signal:** GP on the live chart is currently in Stage 4 — declining from 274 peak

---

## How to Identify the Stage (Step by Step)

1. **Add 30-week MA (150-day MA) to TradingView chart**
2. **Check price vs MA:**
   - Price above rising MA → Stage 2
   - Price below declining MA → Stage 4
   - Price near flat MA → Stage 1 or Stage 3
3. **Check volume pattern:**
   - More volume on up days → accumulation (Stage 1 → 2)
   - More volume on down days → distribution (Stage 3 → 4)
4. **Check price structure:**
   - Higher highs + higher lows → Stage 2
   - Lower highs + lower lows → Stage 4
   - Flat range → Stage 1 or 3

---

## Entry Rules (Stage 2 Breakout)

- **Entry point:** First close above Stage 1 resistance level
- **Volume requirement:** Volume must be at least 50% above the 10-day average volume on breakout day
- **Stop loss:** Just below the breakout level (1-2% below resistance turned support)
- **Do not chase:** If price is already 5%+ above the breakout point, WAIT for a pullback

---

## Exit Rules

- **Sell signal 1:** Price closes below the 30-week MA after a Stage 2 advance
- **Sell signal 2:** 30-week MA begins to flatten after rising
- **Sell signal 3:** Stock underperforms the market significantly during a market rally
- **Hard stop:** Never let a 15%+ gain turn into a loss — trail your stop
